Research Study: AI Trust in Healthcare

Bayesian Trust Calibration in AI-Assisted Medical Decision Making

Study Instructions

What You'll Do

In this study, you'll review medical scenarios where an AI system provides diagnostic recommendations. Your task is to:

  1. Review each medical case - Read patient symptoms and medical history
  2. Examine AI recommendations - See what the AI system suggests and its confidence level
  3. Rate your trust - Indicate how much you trust the AI's recommendation
  4. Make decisions - Choose whether you would follow the AI's recommendation
  5. Update your opinion - See additional evidence and update your trust rating

Key Points

  • There are no "right" or "wrong" answers - we want your honest opinions
  • AI confidence levels represent the system's uncertainty about its recommendation
  • You can change your trust ratings as you see new evidence
  • Consider both the AI's recommendation and your own medical knowledge/experience

Example Trust Scale

You'll use a scale from 0 (completely distrust) to 100 (complete trust)

0 25 50 75 100
No Trust Low Trust Moderate Trust High Trust Complete Trust
